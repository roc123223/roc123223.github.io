Sevices

At Temple Inn & Suites, we provide many services and accomidations, such as:

    General
  • We have a free contential breakfast for hotel guest, made fresh daily.
  • We have a swimming pool that is open Monday-Saturday, 10am-10pm. Also included are a jacuzzi and showers.
  • Each room has a shower, with the suites having an added bathtub.
  • Each hotel is no more than 5 miles away from the temple it supports, so there is never a far distance!
  • We also allow non-temple goers to stay at this hotel, but the price will be significantly more expensive. We're not discriminating, we're only making sure prices for temple paterons and service mission stay at a low cost.
      • Weddings
  • We provide a reception hall for those who are getting married at the temple in that area. The hall has a kitchen, sound system, and bathrooms.
  • We provide a local catering company for weddings. Once we get in contact with about your reservation we will give you the catering companies' information.
  • We provide a couple of honeymoon suites for the newly-weds, but they are are limited. Please make sure you reserve your room well in advace before the wedding.
      • Full-Time Missionary Service
  • We also provide suites for full-time temple missionaries. These suites have all of the same appliances, bathroom, kitchen, and more of a normal, one bed/one bath apartment.
  • Rates go from $300 to $1,000 a month depending on size of suite and length of stay. Fill out a reservation form and we'll give you a quote of your stay.
  • The continental breakfast is part of your rent and will still be provided to you.
