Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites!

Since 2019, Temple Inn & Suites has provided many temple patrons with quality, affordable housing for their temple stays. For many people who want to attend the temple, the trip can take many days, and man miles. And once they do make it, they cannot afford a standard hotel. That is where we come in. Those who are traveling from very far away can book a great hotel that costs significantly less than normal hotels. we hope you enjoy using our site, and may your temple experiance be holy and divine!

About Us

Temple Inn & Suites was founded on April 10th, 2019, by Robert Willardson. Robert has been a member all of his life, and was raised in rural Utah. As such, he always had a temple no less than one mile away from his home. One day he was reading in the scriptures about temple service, when the sadding thought came to him that many faithful latter-day saints have to travel many miles to go to the temple; some may only ever go once, to recieve their own ordiances. So, Robert decided to do something about it, and now we have the Temple Inn & Suites corporation.
